About

Done it twice.
Ready to do it again.

I am a cybersecurity marketing executive with a track record of building go-to-market functions from early stage through successful exit. Not once, but twice. My work spans category creation, demand generation, analyst relations, ABM, and M&A integration, always in service of one goal: turning a great security product into a market-leading brand.

Most recently, I served as VP of Marketing at Fortra, leading the $200M+ ARR portfolio of digital risk and email protection solutions. Before that, as VP of Marketing at PhishLabs, I helped scale from under $2M to $26M ARR and built the foundation that led to Fortra's acquisition. Earlier at Secureworks, I was part of the team that grew from $8M to over $100M ARR ahead of the Dell acquisition. Both exits taught me how to move fast with lean resources, align marketing tightly with sales, and build brand equity that compounds.

I thrive in the GTM build-out phase, when the strategy needs to be invented, not inherited. If you are a cybersecurity company ready to scale, I would love to talk.
